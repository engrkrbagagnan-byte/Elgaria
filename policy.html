Our Commitment to You

Shipping & Delivery

We ensure secure and timely delivery for every timepiece.

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Delivery is handled by trusted couriers and tracking information will be emailed once your order ships.

  • Metro Manila: 2–4 business days.
  • Luzon: 3–7 business days.
  • Visayas & Mindanao: 5–10 business days.

Please inspect your package upon delivery and report any shipping damage within 48 hours.

Returns & Exchanges

Your satisfaction is important to us.

Returns are accepted within 7 days from delivery for unused items only. The product must include all original packaging, tags, and protective films. Please contact support to request a return authorization and instructions.

Return shipping is the responsibility of the buyer unless the return is due to a confirmed seller error. We recommend using an insured courier and retaining tracking information.

Product Warranty

We stand behind the craftsmanship of our watches.

Our limited warranty covers manufacturing defects for 7 days from delivery. It does not cover damage resulting from water exposure (unless specified), scratches, misuse, or accidental damage. To file a warranty claim, provide clear photo or video evidence taken at the moment of unboxing and contact our support team.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy.

We collect only the information necessary to process orders and communicate with you. We do not sell your personal data. For full details, or to request deletion or export of your information, contact our support team.
